Nacpan Beach Destination

Nacpan Beach is located in the vicinity of El Nido in Palawan, Philippines. It is one of the Philippines' most exquisite beaches. The kind of place you'd picture if you closed your eyes and envisioned paradise, with four kilometers of soft cream-colored sand, swaying coconut palms, a fishing town, beautiful aqua water, and small surfable waves.

How To Go There?

Nacpan Beach is located about a 45-minute drive from El Nido town. Along the way, travelers can make short stops at scenic spots such as small waterfalls, adding more charm to the trip. There are several ways to reach the beach, depending on your preference for comfort, speed, and adventure.

Tuktuk

You can negotiate a tuk-tuk ride for a slower, more local experience, though it may cost more.

Shuttle

Comfortable shuttle vans run daily from El Nido town, usually with hotel pick-up and drop-off. A round trip costs about ₱600.

Moped

Rent a moped for around ₱500 a day (or ₱250 for half a day). It's the most flexible option and lets you explore at your own pace—just don't forget your helmet!
